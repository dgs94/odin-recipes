Coleslaw

Description

This coleslaw takes no time at all to prepare. It's a light and sweet and touch of acidity. The perfect complement to any BBQ dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag pre-shredded cabbage mix
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together mayo, honey, lemon juice, vinegar, salt and pepper until the mixture is combined and without lumps.
  2. Place half the cabbage in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Pour half the dressing over the cabbage in the large bowl.
  4. Add the remaining cabbage mix and pour the remaning dressing over it.
  5. Stir all until combined and cabbage is evenly coated in dressing.
  6. Chill the mix at least 1 hr in the fridge.
  7. When ready to serve, stir and garnish with salt and pepper.

Cornbread

Description

Crispy edges with a soft and fluffy interior. This golden, buttery cornbread is the perfect side.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Cornmeal
  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup buttermilk (room temp)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400deg F. Grease 10" cast iron pan and set aside.
  2. Whisk the cornmeal, flour, baking powder, baking sode and salt in a large bowl. Set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk the melted butter, brown sugar and honey until smooth and thick.
  4. Whisk in the egg until combined. Then whisk in the buttermilk.
  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until combined. Be careful not to overmix!
  6. Pour the batter in the cast iron skillet and bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown on top and centre is cooked through.
  7. Remove from oven and allow to slightly cool before slicing and serving.
